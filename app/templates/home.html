What is SeRAJ?

SeRAJ is a room reservation system where professors and their TAs can request to reserve rooms, computer sites and conference rooms. These requests will be processed by the educational administratives.

If you already have an account, go ahead and log in. If you are new to SeRAJ, contact your department educational administratives or your professor in order to get an account..
